How It Works

This checker matches your symptoms to a curated dataset and shows likely conditions with a match score.

It supports typed and selected symptoms, handles multi‑word phrases (e.g., “shortness of breath”), and shows precautions from the dataset. Not a diagnostic tool.

What We Analyze

We compare your typed or selected symptoms to our dataset. Examples include:

  • Respiratory symptoms (cough, shortness of breath, sore throat)
  • General symptoms (fever, fatigue, body ache)
  • Gastrointestinal symptoms (nausea, stomach pain, vomiting, diarrhoea)
  • Neurological symptoms (headache, dizziness)
  • And more via custom input

Results are ranked by match % and include practical precautions.

Our Mission

We aim to provide accessible health insights to help people make informed decisions about their health. By leveraging AI technology, we make symptom analysis available 24/7, helping users understand when they should seek medical attention.

Our goal is to bridge the gap between symptom awareness and professional medical care, empowering users with knowledge while emphasizing the importance of consulting healthcare professionals.
